Klara Pricing

At Klara, we believe in a pricing model that is as flexible and transparent as our service. We offer a single, straightforward pricing plan designed to align directly with the value you receive.

Pay-As-You-Go

Our core philosophy is "use as much as you want, pay as you go." This means:

  • No Fixed Monthly Fees: You won't be charged a recurring fee just for having an account.
  • No Upfront Credit Purchases: Forget about buying credits in advance or worrying about unused allocations.
  • Consumption-Based: Your investment is tied directly to your actual usage. You only pay for the intelligence you utilize. This approach ensures affordability and accessibility for everyone, from individual professionals to large enterprises. Critically, we do not bill fixed fees *per user* or *per seat*. In a world where many enterprise solutions impose fixed monthly, per-seat charges, this often results in significant wasted expenditure due to fluctuating utilization and varying levels of consumption. With Klara, you only pay for what you genuinely use, removing financial barriers and allowing you to grant access to every team member without prohibitive per-user costs.

How Your Costs Are Calculated

To provide you with precise and realistic "Pay-As-You-Go" costs, our pricing is calculated at a granular level based on three primary components, with distinct unit prices for our two available plans.

  • Base AI Plan: Uses standard AI models (e.g., Gemini Flash 2.5) for everyday tasks, efficiency, and general knowledge.
  • Pro AI Plan: Employs professional AI models (e.g., Gemini Pro 2.5) for enhanced capabilities, complex problem-solving, and advanced reasoning.

This granular approach offers excellent control and value for every unit of service you use.

While our standard model offers complete flexibility, we understand that you may desire enhanced predictability and assurance. You can easily set up guaranteed alerts and specific controls over your usage directly within your Settings. This allows you to manage and monitor your consumption according to your precise requirements.
